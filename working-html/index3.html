Did Connecticut Stay Blue?

By Joelle Anselmo

The midterm elections just happened and Connecticut's track record of voting Democrat has continued for another year. Historically, New England states tend to vote blue and Connecticut is no exeption. The most populated county in the state, Fairfield County, pushed forward the vote for Democrat candidates, while the counties of Litchfield and Windham, who normally vote red, cannot seem to get their voices heard in a predominantly blue state.
